Who am i ?

I am a young artist based in the Chicagoland area. I am always ready for my next bigger and crazier project!

I love getting out of my comfort zone when it comes to art, from trying different techniques to painting something I would not normally paint.

my inspiration

Although, my inspiration has mainly derived from plants and animals because they are full of life, I do not limit myself to creating artwork with that.

Did I mention, I love traveling! Traveling always brings me closer to fresh ideas to the "Down Under" and many places in between!

Travel

Traveling always brings me closer to fresh ideas for my artwork that I continue to create.

I am eager to create something bigger and better each time.
