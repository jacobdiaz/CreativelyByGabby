Design Research

Build a more robust technology enabling student and faculty efficiency and involvement post-pandemic at universities?

The Ah-Where system embedded throughout universities is a data-tracking system that encompasses several mediums of use, from in the moment to the future. It embraces efficiency and convenience. The Ah-Where system is composed of already existing technologies like card-swiping, but also introduces sensors to fill any data collection gaps. This granular data will not only inform future critical decisions for being carbon neutral but will also provide live updates of capacity, cleanliness and reserving space to communication platforms already used by universities.

This concept was developed based on primary and secondary research conducted within an interdisciplinary team-based class at the University of Illinois at Chicago. Various design research methods were implemented, including market research, user persona development, strategic surveying and human-human interviewing.

Build an easy-to-use supplemental app for museum viewers to engage with at their convenience? This app works as an addition to informational graphics within a museum.
